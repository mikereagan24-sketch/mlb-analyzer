MLB Analyzer — Actuals Upload Bookmarklet (canonical source)
=============================================================

This is the bookmarklet Mike uses to refresh actuals from FanGraphs.
Active in his browser as a saved bookmark; this file is the canonical
reference so future debugging knows the exact URL parameters being
sent.

Key parameter: autoPt=false (disables FG's automatic PA-qualifying
threshold). Required to surface low-usage relievers like Bennett Sousa
who would otherwise be filtered out, leaving 30-80% of MLB bullpen
tail pitchers absent from pit-act-* rows.

Usage: paste the entire `javascript:...` line below into the URL
field of a browser bookmark. To run: navigate to any fangraphs.com
page, then click the bookmark.
